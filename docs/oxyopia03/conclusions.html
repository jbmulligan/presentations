Conclusions

                       Eye movement latencies are influenced by simple stimulus manipulations,
                       in ways similar to perception.

                       The method provides fine time scale information about visual processing
                       not easily obtained from traditional psychophysical methods.


             Future Work

                       Get the pursuit model to fit exactly.

                       Incorporate a model of the saccadic system.

                       Continue to explore the space of stimuli.

                       Develop "multifocal" stimulus.
