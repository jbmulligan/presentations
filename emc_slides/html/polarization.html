Polarization analysis


                       The vertical-horizontal asymmetry presumably arises from asymmetry
                       in the visual and/or oculomotor systems, the "physiological axes."

                       These may be fortuitously aligned with our stimulator and tracker
                       coordinate systems, but can we measure them?

                       The stimulatus and eye movement trajectories may be re-expressed in
                       rotated coordinate systems.

                       We expect HIGH correlations between target motion in a given direction
                       and eye motion in the SAME direction;

                       We expect LOW correlations between target motion in a given direction
                       and eye motion in the ORTHOGONAL direction;
